Welcome to the AI Music Co-pilot!

This is your guide to creating music with your AI partner. The AI is context-aware, meaning it remembers what's playing and makes intelligent suggestions. You can build a track layer by layer, just by talking to it.

Getting Started: Your First Track in 3 Steps

  1. Start with a beat: Try "create a simple house beat" or "give me a fast techno rhythm".
  2. Add a bassline: Try "add a funky bassline that fits". The AI will listen to the beat and match the vibe.
  3. Add harmony: Try "add some chill chords" or "create an epic pad progression in C minor".

ü•Å The Drum Machine

You can ask for drums using natural language, or use this special syntax for precise control: play drums "[pattern]". The pattern characters are:

  • x - Kick Drum
  • o - Snare Drum
  • h - Closed Hi-Hat
  • H - Open Hi-Hat
  • c - Clap
  • t - Tom Drum
  • - or . - Rest (silence)

Example: play drums "x-H-c-H-" creates a classic house groove.

üéπ Melodic Instruments

For bass, leads, or pads, use this syntax: play [type] [notes] as [preset].

  • Type: Can be bass, lead, or pads.
  • Notes: A list of numbers like [0, 4, 7]. These work like piano keys, where 0 is the root note (C), 1 is C#, 2 is D, etc. Negative numbers work too!
  • Preset: Defines the sound. Try saw-lead, sine-pad, or square-bass.

Example: play pads [0, 4, 7] as sine-pad creates a beautiful C Major chord with a soft pad sound.
