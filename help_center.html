Help Center

Have questions? We've got answers. Find what you're looking for in our FAQ below.

What is AI Music Co-pilot?

AI Music Co-pilot is a web-based application that allows you to create music using natural language. You purchase our professional software once, connect your own free Google AI API key, and you can create limitlessly.

Why do I need my own Google AI API Key?

The "Bring Your Own Key" model gives you maximum power and privacy. By using your own key, you get unlimited generations (subject to Google's generous free tier) and your creative prompts are sent directly from your browser to Google, not through our servers. You are in full control. You can get a free key from Google AI Studio.

How does the Lifetime Deal work?

Our Lifetime Deal is a one-time purchase that gives you permanent access to the AI Music Co-pilot application and all future software updates. There are no subscriptions. Your only ongoing cost is the API usage from Google, which is free for most users under their generous free tier.

Is my API key secure?

Yes. Your API key is stored securely in your browser's local storage and is never sent to our servers. It's only used by your computer to communicate directly with the Google AI API.

Can I export my music?

Audio export functionality is a planned feature for a future update. Our goal is to allow you to export your creations as high-quality WAV or MP3 files.
